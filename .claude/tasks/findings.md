# Research Findings

## LLM Council — 2026-06-04

### Question
Design a website for early-stage Indian consumer VCs: taxonomy for funding rounds, top-50 brand dashboard, and additional applet sections.

### Council ranking
Domain Rigorist (1.0) > Red-teamer (2.2) > First-principles (3.2) > Pragmatist (3.8) > Generalist (4.8)

### Key findings adopted

**Taxonomy:**
- 3-axis structure (Sector × GTM × Income tier) is the cleanest MECE
- QC = distribution tag, NOT a sector (universal agreement)
- India 1/2/3 tier overlay essential for VCs to distinguish market positioning

**Signals:**
- Meta Ads: no spend data in India (political ads only); rewards burn; high-velocity + low-brand-search = negative flag
- Google Trends: relative 0-100, slope only, useless for sub-scale brands
- QC pincode coverage: strongest India-specific signal; but presence ≠ velocity
- Review accretion rate (not absolute count) is the better review signal
- Sub-10k-follower UGC = earned affinity vs. bought reach
- Z-score within sector cohort for normalization
- LinkedIn headcount: lags; function mix (ops/supply-chain) > total

**Sections (priority order):**
1. Funding Rounds Explorer (spine for all other sections)
2. Momentum Dashboard (hero — re-weightable composite score)
3. Category White-space Map (find the gaps)
4. Investor Activity / Co-investment (same data, new pivot)
5. Graduation Funnel (novel — seed→Series A/B conversion by sector)
6. Unit Economics Sandbox (highest decision-value per Red-teamer + First-principles)

**Critical meta-point (Red-teamer):**
All-dummy data is the most commoditized artifact in Indian consumer VC. Must seed with 15-20 real, verifiable brands and real (manually-curated, point-in-time) public signal data. Fake-live kills credibility; real-but-dated earns it.
